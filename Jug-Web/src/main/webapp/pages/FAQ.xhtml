ESPRIT JUG

JUG stands for Java User Group is an independent, international, non-profit organization that bring together stake holders and actors who share an interest in the Java technology.ESPRIT JUG is the first JUG organization active in Tunisia. We are a group of teachers scientists, engineers, professionals and students who aims to promote the Java technology through conferences regularly. ESPRIT JUG hopes to play an important role in promoting the Java technology spikes in Tunisia.

How do I apply for a presentation ?

Anyone can apply to make a presentation at ESPRIT JUG. To do this, you must first create a profile presenter in the "" site. Then complete the form to describe your presentation. It is possible to provide several presentations on different topics or different formats. A selection will then be made ​​by the Program Committee. Then we will tell you if your proposal has been accepted or not. You can send us proposals for presentations are chosen by the organizers...
